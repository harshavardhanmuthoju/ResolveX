Project Brief

Why ResolveX exists

Zero Bottleneck

Complaints go straight to the relevant academic department instead of waiting in a central queue.

Direct Accountability

HODs cannot claim a complaint was unseen after opening it because the system records acknowledgment instantly.

Student Transparency

Every status transition is visible in a simple history timeline that students can revisit anytime.

Low-End Hardware Friendly

Department-scoped filtering and lean UI patterns keep the experience smooth on modest institutional hardware.

System Design

Frontend, backend, and database responsibilities

Frontend

Role-based React SPA with protected routes for Students and HODs.

  • Student complaint submission and tracking
  • Department-filtered HOD dashboard
  • Status timeline and resolution actions

Backend

Java REST services enforcing JWT-based role and department authorization.

  • Login and registration
  • Complaint CRUD and status updates
  • Read acknowledgment trigger endpoint

Oracle Database

Immutable storage with complaint ordering and append-only status logging.

  • Sequences for chronology
  • Triggers for audit history
  • Indexed department filters
